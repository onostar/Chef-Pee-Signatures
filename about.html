Get to know us

Chef Pee signatures is a Nigerian catering company founded in 2017 by Akponwei Promise. We offer services which range form personal catering service, to weddings, birthdays and anniversaries.
Chef Pee Signatures started as a small home-based business and have expanded year after year. Today we cang boast of a modern kitchen with state of the art equipment.

Our focus is on great food and impeccable service; coupled with our deeply rooted health and safety policy. We understand the uniqueness of all our clients hence; we tailor our services to suit the specific needs of each client.
Our secret in managing our success is simple. We build long lasting relationships with our clients.

Chef pee signatures offers excellent service that no other catering company can replicate. Our solid reputation and impressive clients list is a reflection of our success in this mission. Our level of dedication and quality of service sets us apart from the rest.
